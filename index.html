Preface

Welcome to my personal webpage.

I am a post-doctoral researcher working on human genomics.

In this page there is information about my research and my projects.

 

A brief summary

The main theme of my research is human genetics based on bioinformatic analysis,
with focus mainly on population and medical genetics.

My research centers on:

  • Genetics of complex diseases and population genetics.
  • Genomic and epigenomic large scale data analysis.
  • Analysis of common and rare genomic variants implicated in disease.
  • Identification of population structure and stratification.
  • Archaeogenetics

Background

I studied Molecular Biology and Genetics at Democritus University of Thrace.

I did my PhD primarily at Democritus University and at Purdue University,
working in the Laboratory of Human Genome Variation under the supervision of Prof. Peristera Paschou and Prof. Petros Drineas.
