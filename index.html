A brief summary

The main theme of my research is human genetics based on bioinformatic analysis, with focus mainly on population and medical genetics.

My research centers on:

  • Genetics of complex diseases and population genetics.
  • Genomic and epigenomic large scale data analysis.
  • Analysis of common and rare genomic variants implicated in disease.
  • Identification of population structure and stratification.
  • Archaeogenetics

I did my PhD primarily at Democritus University of Thrace and at Purdue University, working in the Laboratory of Human Genome Variation under the supervision of Prof. Peristera Paschou.
