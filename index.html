My Personal Webpage Preface Welcome to my personal webpage. I am a post-doctoral researcher working on human genomics. In this page there is information about my research and my projects. A brief summary The main theme of my research is human genetics based on bioinformatic analysis, with focus mainly on population and medical genetics. My research centers on: Genetics of complex diseases and population genetics. Genome-wide association studies, exome sequencing, whole genome sequencing and large scale data analysis. Analysis of common and rare genomic variants implicated in disease. Identification of population structure and stratification. I did my PhD primarily at Democritus University of Thrace and at Purdue University, working in the Laboratory of Human Genome Variation under the supervision of Prof. Peristera Paschou.
